Satellite Clinics

With two additional satellite clinics {{site.name}} strategy is to move services closer to the community, meeting the people’s needs where they live and work.

Our satellite clinic service delivery points are situated in the densely populated cities of Ibadan (Yemetu) and Ogbomosho (Ijeru) in Oyo State to provide both male and female clients with RH/FP services, HIV/AIDS Counselling and Testing, and Referrals.

Both clinics operate within the hours of 8am and 5pm week days. Regularly, outreach activities are carried out in markets and communities to sensitise buyers, sellers, trade groups and community members to the importance of patronising Sexual and Reproductive Health ({{site.name}})) Centres.

The clinics are staffed with qualified health personnel, comprising Nurses, Medical Laboratory Scientists and Laboratory Assistants.

Services provided include:

  • Intra Uterine Contraceptive Device (IUCD)
  • Injectables - Depo provera and Noristeriat
  • Oral contraceptive Pills
  • Female and Male condoms
