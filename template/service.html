HCSI EMPOWERNMENT

The Tunde and Ebun Delano Library ({{site.name}}Library), also located within {{site.name}}House premise, has been found very useful as a strategy of inculcating the reading culture into them and reaching them with SRH/FP and HIV/AIDS information.

To further enhance the aim of the library, skill building programmes are organised weekly for the library users. Topics discussed include Time Management, Study Skills Building , Sexual Reproductive Health Hints, Dangers of Substance Abuse, Choosing a Career, Refusal Skills and Relationships, among others. The computerized library enhances easy storage and retrieval of data.

The library is automated to enhance easy storage and retrieval of information. Internet searches are also done in the library to complement physical books and other printed materials. The library operates between 9am and 5pm every working day, and its membership is open to secondary school students, undergraduates, postgraduates and researchers at a reasonable fee.

The Eugene Weiss Internet Browsing Centre

Eugene Weiss Internet Browsing Centre

Since its inception in March 2004, the Eugene Weiss Internet Browsing Centre has been a transformative hub for youths, students, and researchers, bridging the digital divide by providing access to the Internet and vital computer literacy programs.

Empowering Youths Through Computer Literacy

The centre introduced a 6-week computer training programme designed to equip youths with essential skills such as:

  • Computer Appreciation
  • Windows Operating Systems
  • Microsoft Office Applications
  • Desktop Publishing

This program, funded by a generous take-off grant from the Augustine Stewardship Trust Fund, Wisconsin, USA, has helped countless participants build a foundation for future success in a digital world.

Internet Access

The centre serves as a resource for students and researchers, offering high-speed Internet access to support learning, research, and personal development.

Photocopying & Scanning

Services like photocopying, scanning, and document processing help students and professionals manage their academic and administrative needs efficiently.

Typing Services

Typing services assist individuals in creating professional documents, assignments, and publications with precision and accuracy.

Training for Digital Success

Beyond the basics, the centre continues to promote advanced digital skills and encourage innovation among youths.

Healthcare & Support Organizations for Children

These organizations are dedicated to improving the health, education, and well-being of children. From hospitals to community care centers, they provide essential services to ensure a brighter future for every child.

  • Civil Societies on HIV/AIDS in Nigeria (CISHAN)

    Providing support and education to children and families affected by HIV/AIDS.

  • St. Philomena’s Catholic Hospital

    Offers pediatric care and immunization programs for children.

  • Faith Mediplex

    Specializes in maternal and child health, ensuring safe deliveries and healthy growth.

  • University of Benin Teaching Hospital (UBTH)

    Provides advanced pediatric care and training programs for healthcare workers.

  • Central Hospital Benin

    A center for vaccinations, child nutrition, and healthcare services.

  • Ministry of Women Affairs, Edo State

    Promotes education and healthcare programs for disadvantaged children.
